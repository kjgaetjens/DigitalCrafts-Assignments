Wafer Vs Cookie

Are they really that different?

The only real similarity between the Nutter Butter wafer and Nutter Butter cookie is the creme filling. While this might seem like the only important component, it's not. The pairing of the external shell with the internal creme makes an enormous different. It affects taste, texture, and experience.
